About Me

Passionate about bringing imagination to life

My name is Kartik Parmar and I'm a visual effects artist living in Gujarat state of India. I am currently pursuing Advanced VFX from Maya Academy of Advanced Cinematics, Mumbai, IN.

I've been exploring the visual effects industry for last five years and have worked on various interesting projects across a varied VFX domains. My major interests lie in the fields of VFX composition and concept art.

The recent shortfilm named 'Cold Heart' which was edited by me got shortlisted in the Indian Film House award show in the categories of Best Editing and Best Cinematography.

Location: Vijalpore, Navsari, Gujarat, IN
Education: Advanced VFX from Maya Academy of Advanced Cinematics, Mumbai
